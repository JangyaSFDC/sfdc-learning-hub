About SFDC Learning Hub

Empowering Salesforce professionals with quality tutorials and practical guides

Our Mission

SFDC Learning Hub was created with a simple mission: to make Salesforce knowledge accessible to everyone. Whether you're a beginner just starting your Salesforce journey or an experienced developer looking to sharpen your skills, our platform provides comprehensive, practical, and up-to-date content.

We believe in learning by doing. That's why every tutorial includes real-world examples, code snippets you can use in your projects, and best practices that reflect industry standards.

What We Cover

Apex Development

Triggers, batch processing, queueable apex, test classes, design patterns, and governor limits.

Lightning Web Components

Component architecture, wire services, events, SLDS integration, and performance optimization.

Admin & Configuration

Security model, data management, declarative automation, reports, and dashboards.

Integration

REST/SOAP APIs, platform events, change data capture, external services, and middleware.

Flows & Automation

Screen flows, record-triggered flows, scheduled flows, and automation best practices.

Interview Preparation

Commonly asked questions, scenario-based problems, and tips for Salesforce interviews.

Why Choose Us?

There are many Salesforce learning resources available, but here's what sets us apart:

  • Practical Focus — Every article is designed with real-world application in mind
  • Up-to-Date Content — We keep our tutorials current with the latest Salesforce releases
  • Beginner Friendly — Complex concepts are explained in simple, easy-to-understand language
